Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Voyager Map mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and protects personal data. The App helps travelers find free public water fountains and Wi-Fi hotspots, and manage their travel plans and saved destinations.

It is needed to be updated, when registration will be avaible for customers.

1. Data Controller

The App is operated by a private individual:

  • Developer: Voyager Map (individual developer)
  • Contact: voyagemapsolution@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal data through social login or in-app usage:

  • Full name (from social login)
  • Email address (from social login)
  • Profile picture URL (from social login)
  • Social login ID (Facebook, Google, Apple)
  • Saved travel destinations and preferences
  • Billing data (for payment-related services)
  • Usage statistics (for feature development)
  • Advertising analytics (Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics, TikTok Pixel)

3. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Enable login via Facebook, Google, or Apple
  • Store and retrieve user profile data
  • Enable invoicing (if purchases occur)
  • Improve the App based on usage patterns
  • Display ads and measure their effectiveness

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure backend servers. Storage is currently managed via virtual private servers; future infrastructure may include Azure PostgreSQL or container-based cloud storage.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We may share your data with the following third parties for functional and legal purposes:

  • Facebook, Google, Apple – to authenticate your login
  • Analytics and ad networks – to measure performance (Facebook, Google, TikTok)
  • Billing provider – for invoicing, if applicable

6. Data Retention

We retain your data until your account is deleted. If you request data deletion, your account data will be fully anonymized and irreversibly removed from our systems.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Object to data processing

To exercise your rights, contact us at: voyagemapsolution@gmail.com

8. Data Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored securely.

9. External Services and Links

The App may contain links to external services (e.g. map providers, analytics, ads). These services operate under their own privacy policies.

10. Child Protection

We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. Our App is not intended for children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16 without parental consent. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 16 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Parents or guardians who believe that their child has provided us with personal data without their consent should contact us at voyagemapsolution@gmail.com. We will take reasonable steps to delete such information from our records.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated in the App or via email, where appropriate.
