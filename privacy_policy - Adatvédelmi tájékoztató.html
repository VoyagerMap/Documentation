Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Voyager Map mobile application (the “App”) processes personal data. The App provides travel-related features such as discovering points of interest, saving destinations, planning trips, tracking costs, sharing trips with other users, and related communication features.

1. Data Controller

The App is operated by an individual developer:

  • Developer: Voyager Map (individual developer)
  • Contact email: voyagemapsolution@gmail.com

2. Categories of Personal Data

  • Account and profile data: name, email address, profile image URL (via social login), social login identifiers (Facebook, Google, Apple), authentication metadata.
  • Optional profile details: phone number, address data, date of birth, gender.
  • Location data (with permission): device location for map and nearby features, including one-time or active tracking while a feature is enabled.
  • Travel planning data: saved locations, itineraries, notes, preferences.
  • Trip cost data: expense entries and cost-related information provided by you.
  • Sharing and collaboration data: shared trip content, permissions, activity data.
  • User-generated content: custom POIs, uploaded images or files, visibility depending on your sharing and privacy settings.
  • Guest-mode data (where applicable): guest identifiers, device and technical data, temporary preferences and content.
  • Feedback and bug reports: submitted feedback and diagnostic information.
  • Analytics and technical data: in-app events, device and app data, IP address, timestamps, SDK-generated identifiers.
  • Advertising and attribution data (where applicable): identifiers and event data processed subject to legal requirements.
  • Billing and transaction data (where applicable): records required for invoicing and accounting.

3. Purposes and Legal Bases of Processing

  • Account creation and authentication – performance of a contract
  • Core app functionality – performance of a contract
  • Trip sharing initiated by you – performance of a contract
  • Location-based features – performance of a contract and device permissions
  • User communication via email (service-related notifications, feature updates, security information) – performance of a contract and legitimate interest
  • Customer support and security – legitimate interest
  • Guest-mode operation – legitimate interest
  • Billing and legal compliance – legal obligation
  • Product analytics and improvement (including PostHog) – legitimate interest
  • Advertising and marketing measurement – user consent, where required

4. Email Communication

We may occasionally contact you via email in connection with the operation of the App, including important service-related information, security notices, feature updates, or changes affecting your use of the App. Marketing or promotional emails are sent only where permitted by law and, where required, based on your consent. You may opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

5. PostHog Analytics and Product Tools

The App uses PostHog to understand product usage and improve reliability and user experience. Depending on configuration, PostHog may be used for analytics, feature flags, surveys, and limited session replay functionality. Reasonable measures are applied to reduce the likelihood of capturing sensitive information in analytics data or recordings.

6. Advertising and Measurement Tools

The App may use Google, Meta, and TikTok tools for analytics, attribution, and advertising measurement, subject to applicable legal requirements and user consent where required.

7. Server Logs and Diagnostics

Server logs may record technical data such as request timestamps, IP address, user-agent, and error information for operational and security purposes.

8. Data Storage and Infrastructure

Personal data is stored on backend infrastructure operated by the developer. Uploaded files may be stored in object storage systems and referenced via stored URLs.

9. Data Sharing with Third Parties

Data may be shared only as necessary with authentication providers, analytics services, advertising partners, billing providers, or storage providers. Third parties process data under their own policies.

10. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary. Upon account deletion, personal data is deleted or anonymized unless legal obligations require retention.

11. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request rectification or erasure
  • Request restriction or object to processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Requests can be submitted to voyagemapsolution@gmail.com.

12. Data Security

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are applied to protect personal data.

13. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 16. Data of children is not knowingly processed without parental consent.

14. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Changes will be communicated within the App or by other appropriate means.
