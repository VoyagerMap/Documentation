Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Voyager Map mobile application (the “App”) processes personal data. The App helps travelers discover relevant points of interest and supports travel planning features including saved destinations, itineraries, trip cost tracking, and sharing trips with other users.

1. Data Controller

The App is operated by an individual developer:

  • Developer: Voyager Map (individual developer)
  • Contact email: voyagemapsolution@gmail.com

2. Categories of Personal Data

The App may process the following categories of personal data:

  • Account and profile data: name, email address, profile image URL (provided via social login), social login identifiers (Facebook, Google, Apple), and authentication method metadata (e.g., linked login methods).
  • Contact and profile details you provide (optional): phone number, address details (country/state/city/ZIP/street/house number), date of birth, and gender.
  • Location data (when you grant permission): device location (e.g., GPS) to enable map-related features and “near me” functionality. Depending on features you use, the App may process one-time location access and/or continuous location tracking while a tracking feature is active.
  • Travel planning data: saved locations, saved destinations, preferences, itineraries, notes, and other travel-related content you create in the App.
  • Trip cost data: expense entries and cost-related information you input.
  • Sharing and collaboration data: information required to share trips with other users (such as shared trip content, access permissions, and related activity).
  • User-generated content (where applicable): content you upload or create, such as custom points of interest and uploaded images/files. If you mark content as public or share it, it may become visible to other users according to your settings.
  • Guest-mode data (where applicable): guest identifiers and related technical data used to operate the App without a registered account (e.g., guest ID, device-related identifiers, IP address, user agent), plus guest preferences/activities and temporary guest-created points (if available in the feature set).
  • Feedback and bug reports (where applicable): user feedback you submit, and diagnostic information related to errors or malfunctions (which may include device/app details, timestamps, and error messages).
  • Analytics and technical data: in-app events and interactions, device and app information (e.g., device model, OS version, app version), approximate network-related information (e.g., IP address), timestamps, and technical identifiers required to operate analytics and measure performance (e.g., SDK-generated identifiers).
  • Advertising and attribution data (where applicable): identifiers and event information processed to measure advertising effectiveness, attribution, and conversions via third-party tools (for example, advertising identifiers and measurement event data), subject to applicable legal requirements.
  • Billing and transaction-related data (where applicable): billing details and records required for invoicing and accounting. (Payment processing may be handled by third-party providers.)

3. Purposes and Legal Bases of Processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes and legal bases:

  • Account creation and authentication – performance of a contract
  • Providing core app functionality (saving places, itineraries, cost tracking) – performance of a contract
  • Trip sharing features (enabling user-to-user sharing you initiate) – performance of a contract
  • Location-based features (map and nearby results, tracking features when enabled) – performance of a contract; and device permission management
  • Customer support, feedback handling, and security – legitimate interest
  • Maintaining guest-mode functionality (where available) – legitimate interest
  • Billing and invoicing (if purchases occur) – compliance with legal obligations
  • Product analytics and improvement (including PostHog) – legitimate interest
  • Advertising, marketing measurement, attribution, and remarketing (including Google/Meta/TikTok measurement tools) – user consent, where required
  • Surveys and feedback (to improve features and user experience) – legitimate interest (or consent where required)

4. PostHog: Autocapture, Session Replay, Feature Flags, Surveys

The App uses PostHog to understand product usage, improve reliability, and support controlled feature rollouts and experimentation. PostHog features used by the App may include:

  • Autocapture – automatic capture of certain in-app interactions and events
  • Session replay – replay of user sessions to help diagnose issues and improve user experience
  • Feature flags – enabling/disabling features for subsets of users or devices
  • Surveys – presenting optional surveys or feedback prompts within the App

Session replay and event capture may process interaction data and technical information. Where practical, the App may apply measures to reduce the likelihood that sensitive information (such as passwords or payment details) is captured in recordings or analytics events. You should avoid entering sensitive information into free-text fields unless it is required for the relevant feature.

5. Analytics and Advertising Tools (Google / Meta / TikTok)

The App may use analytics, attribution, and advertising measurement tools to evaluate performance and improve campaigns, where applicable:

  • Google – analytics and/or advertising measurement tools (as applicable)
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) – analytics and/or advertising measurement tools (as applicable)
  • TikTok – analytics and/or advertising measurement tools (as applicable)

Where required by law, we rely on your consent for advertising-related measurement, attribution, and remarketing activities. You may withdraw your consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

6. Server Logs and Diagnostics

To operate and secure the service, server-side logs may record technical request and diagnostic information, such as request timestamps, endpoints accessed, response status codes, approximate location derived from IP, IP address, and user-agent string. Error logs may include error messages and diagnostic details (for example, stack traces) to help identify and fix issues.

7. Data Storage and Infrastructure

Personal data is stored on backend infrastructure operated by the developer. The technical environment may change over time and can include virtual private servers or managed cloud database services. Uploaded files (where applicable) may be stored in object storage (e.g., S3-compatible storage) and referenced via stored URLs.

8. Data Sharing with Third Parties

Personal data may be shared with third parties only to the extent necessary for the purposes described above, for example:

  • Authentication providers (Facebook, Google, Apple)
  • Analytics and measurement providers (including PostHog; and Google/Meta/TikTok as applicable)
  • Advertising and attribution partners (where applicable and where legally permitted)
  • Billing or payment service providers (where applicable)
  • File storage or media processing providers used to store or process uploaded content (where applicable)

Third-party services process personal data under their own privacy policies. Voyager Map is not responsible for the data handling practices of such providers.

9. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy. Upon account deletion, personal data will be deleted or irreversibly anonymized, unless further retention is required by applicable law (for example, certain billing records). Server logs and security logs may be retained for a limited period for operational and security purposes.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request rectification of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent

Requests may be submitted via email: voyagemapsolution@gmail.com

11. Data Security

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are applied to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

12. External Services

The App may contain links to or integrate external services (for example maps, analytics, advertising, or other third-party services). These services operate independently and under their own privacy policies.

13. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 16. Personal data of children is not knowingly processed without parental consent. If such data is identified, reasonable steps will be taken to remove it.

14. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be communicated within the App or by other appropriate means.
